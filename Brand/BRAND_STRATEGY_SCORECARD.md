# Café Athena — Brand Strategy Scorecard

**Status:** Active
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-28

---

## Executive Summary

All eight phases of the brand build are complete. Three social channels are live with first content posted as of 2026-06-28 (Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube). The site is deployed and indexed. The manuscript is in production. The work ahead is not brand construction — it is brand execution: building an audience that belongs to this brand specifically, maintaining content quality and voice consistency across surfaces, sustaining a recipe output cadence that serves the three audience personas, and establishing Café Athena's position in the culinary content space as a resource organized around understanding rather than output. These four objectives govern the next twelve months.

---

## Objective 1 — Audience Growth

**Goal:** Build a readership and following across site and social channels that reflects the brand's actual audience — serious home cooks and culinary-curious developing cooks — not a broad general food audience.

| Program / Initiative | KPI / Metric | Target | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly social content (Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube) | Posts per platform per week | 1 reel + 1 supporting post per platform | Ongoing from 2026-06-28 |
| Instagram follower growth | Follower count | 500 by end of Q3 2026 / 2,000 by end of Q4 2026 | Quarterly check |
| Pinterest follower growth | Follower count | 250 by end of Q3 2026 / 1,000 by end of Q4 2026 | Quarterly check |
| YouTube subscriber growth | Subscriber count | 100 by end of Q3 2026 / 500 by end of Q4 2026 | Quarterly check |
| Site traffic (organic) | Monthly sessions, GA4 | 500 sessions/month by end of Q3 2026 / 2,000/month by end of Q4 2026 | Monthly review |
| UTM tracking compliance | % of social links with UTM parameters | 100% of all links from 2026-06-28 forward | Immediate; audit monthly |
| Social-to-site conversion | Sessions attributed to social (GA4) | 15% of total sessions by end of Q4 2026 | Monthly review |

## Objective 2 — Brand Consistency

**Goal:** Maintain voice, visual identity, and positioning across all published surfaces so that every touchpoint — site, social caption, reel, visual asset — is unmistakably Café Athena.

| Program / Initiative | KPI / Metric | Target | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|---|
| Voice audit (acquisition register) | New site copy and social captions reviewed against §6 forbidden phrases before publish | 0 forbidden phrases in any published copy | Every post / every copy update |
| Visual asset compliance (Lane 1) | Recipe hero images meeting §7 standard (warm editorial, correct angle, correct props) | 100% of published recipe heroes | Per-recipe production |
| Visual asset compliance (Lane 2 and social) | Social assets matching brand palette (parchment, charcoal, olive-gold) with correct typography | 100% of produced social assets | Per-asset review |
| Reel voiceover tone | Male Marketing Voice 1 profile — warm, unhurried, no announcer cadence | Pass on first review for every asset; escalate if failing | Per-asset production |
| BRAND_GUIDELINES.md currency | Time elapsed since last review | Reviewed at the start of every session; updated within 48 hours of any brand decision | Ongoing |
| Agent consistency | Writing Director and Brand Manager producing output traceable to brand voice — no AI cadence in published copy | 0 published pieces with AI-pattern language | Every publish cycle |

## Objective 3 — Content Pipeline

**Goal:** Maintain a sustainable recipe and content output cadence that serves all three audience personas and keeps the manuscript moving toward completion.

| Program / Initiative | KPI / Metric | Target | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recipe folio completion rate | New recipe folios registered and deployed per month | 4 folios/month (1/week) | Monthly review |
| Manuscript chapter completion | Chapters with all planned folios complete (per CONTENT_PLAN.md) | 2 additional chapters with full draft coverage by end of Q3 2026 | Quarterly review |

| Part IV (The Expo) development | Capstone chapter progress | First folio outlined by end of Q4 2026 | Q4 milestone |

## Objective 4 — Positioning and Awareness

**Goal:** Establish Café Athena as the culinary resource organized around understanding — specific, technique-forward, honest about difficulty — in contrast to recipe-volume content built for search and sharing.

| Program / Initiative | KPI / Metric | Target | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|---|
| Search visibility — technique queries | Organic ranking for technique-intent queries (e.g. "why pasta water in sauce," "how to properly reduce a sauce") | 3+ technique pages in top 20 for their primary query by end of Q4 2026 | Quarterly audit (Google Search Console) |
| Inbound link development | Backlinks from culinary communities (Reddit, Substack food writing, YouTube descriptions) | 5 inbound links from non-social sources by end of Q4 2026 | Quarterly audit |
| Reddit and culinary community presence | Mentions or links in r/seriouseats, r/AskCulinary, r/cooking, or comparable communities | 2 organic mentions per quarter (not solicited by Kevin) | Quarterly |
| Site engagement depth | Average pages per session, bounce rate — signals whether visitors are exploring beyond entry page | 2.5+ pages/session average; bounce rate under 65% | Monthly (GA4) |
| Positioning language consistency | Every social bio, About page, and channel description uses positioning language consistent with §1 and §6 | 100% consistent across all live surfaces | Audit at each channel launch and quarterly thereafter |
| Brand recall test | Informal: does first-time social content convey "technique-forward, honest, no shortcuts" without stating it explicitly? | Qualitative review of first 10 reels against this test | After first 10 reels are posted |
