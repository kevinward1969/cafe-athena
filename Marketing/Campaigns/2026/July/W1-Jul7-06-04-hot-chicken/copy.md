Nashville Hot Fried Chicken — How the Crust and Glaze Actually Work

**Description:**
The heat in Nashville Hot Chicken comes from a post-fry spice paste — cayenne, brown sugar, and smoked paprika bloomed in hot frying oil — not from the breading. The crust is a Wet-Dry-Wet dredging system: a small pour of buttermilk binder worked back into the dry flour creates the craggy surface the glaze adheres to.
