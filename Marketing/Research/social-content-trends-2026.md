# Social Content Trends — TikTok / Instagram / YouTube

**Source:** Kevin Ward deep research
**Date:** 2026-06-29

---

Most-popular food posts on TikTok/Instagram/YouTube cluster around a few repeatable themes:

## Quick, Visual Recipe Hacks

Short, easy recipes and "food hacks" (pasta chips, smashed potatoes, chopped sandwiches, frozen tomato toast, chickles) that look dramatic on camera and are simple to copy.

One-pan, 3–5‑ingredient, or "lazy" recipes aimed at busy home cooks and students.

## Indulgent Sweets and Desserts

Over-the-top treats like Dubai chocolate bars, kunafa desserts, chaos cakes, cream‑cheese crostini, and loaded pastries.

Aesthetic fruit‑based sweets (Japanese strawberries in cakes, mango pastries, frozen strawberry clusters) that balance "indulgent but slightly healthier."

## Global Street Food and Snacks

Korean street foods (tteokbokki, corn dogs, fried chicken), Southeast Asian street noodles, and Chinese tanghulu fruit skewers.

General "street food" creator content showing vendor dishes, night markets, and travel‑style tasting videos.

## Health, Functional, and "Better-for-You"

Functional drinks and snacks (Sleepy Girl Mocktail, tart cherry juice drinks, protein/Greek‑yogurt treats, matcha lattes) tying taste to sleep, energy, or focus.

Nutrition‑trend posts about high‑protein, gut health, and "intentional eating" routines.

## Eating Experience, ASMR, and Mukbangs

Mukbangs (large-quantity eating) and ASMR eating videos that focus on sound and texture rather than recipes.

"Egg flights," candy tastings, Swedish candy reviews, and similar format where the hook is variety and reaction.

## Lifestyle and "What I Eat" Formats

"What I eat in a day," meal prep, and full‑day food diaries connected to fitness, weight loss, or aesthetic lifestyles.

Budget, college‑dorm, or busy‑parent food days showing realistic eating patterns.

## Reviews, Recommendations, and Food Discovery

Taste‑test and review content for viral products (Dubai chocolate, Swedish candy, matcha drinks) and restaurant menu hacks like the Flying Dutchman burger.

Local restaurant and café discovery clips, often tied to trending items or limited‑time offers.

## Kitchen Tips and Cooking Education

Quick kitchen hacks (air-fryer tricks, chopping methods, storage tips) embedded in recipe videos.

Basic cooking skills and "learn to cook" shorts that ride trend waves while teaching knife skills, seasoning, and plating.
